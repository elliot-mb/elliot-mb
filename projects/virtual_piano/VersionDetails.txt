Improved piano of one octive 

Features of prior version:
	
	- when notes are held, they play for longer than if released immediately
	- multiple notes can be played at once
	- notes show up in text form
	- pre-programmed songs can now be played by holding down the 1 or 2 key
	- when notes are spammed, the sound resets whilst the note has already begun, and not at zero
	this makes for a more seamless repetition of the note 
	- notes held ring for longer 
	- shorter notes fade more naturally

New features/changes made in v0.0.2:

	- added whole higher octave
